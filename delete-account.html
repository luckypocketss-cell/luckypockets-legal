Delete Your LuckyPockets Account

Users may request deletion of their LuckyPockets account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the LuckyPockets app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Select "Delete Account" or contact support.
  4. Your request will be processed and your account will be permanently deleted.

Data deleted

  • Account information
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Reward balance
  • Referral information
  • Survey history
  • Cashout history

Data retention

Certain records may be retained temporarily for fraud prevention, legal obligations, and financial record keeping.
